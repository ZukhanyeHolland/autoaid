Our Pricing

All fees are in South African Rand (ZAR).

Formula: Base Fee + (Distance × Per KM Rate) + (Estimated Time × Rate per Minute) + Any Extras (if applicable)

Example (Towing, 10 km):
Base Fee: R350
Distance Fee: 10 × R14 = R140
Time Fee: 30 × R2.00 = R60
Total: R550
