Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, phone number, ID number.
  • Location Data: GPS/location to facilitate service dispatch.
  • Payment Data: Transaction records via Paystack.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To match Customers with Providers.
  • To process payments and issue receipts.
  • To send service notifications (email/SMS).
  • To improve and personalize our services.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Providers: Your name, phone, and location so they can deliver services.
  • Payment Processors: Paystack for transaction processing.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or court order.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption in transit & at rest) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data via your account settings.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

6. Refund Policy

Cancellations before provider dispatch are fully refundable. Once a Provider is en route or service is rendered, fees are non-refundable. Contact support to request a refund.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date below indicates when changes take effect.
